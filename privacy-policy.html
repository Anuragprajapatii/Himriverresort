Privacy Policy

Him River Resort respects the privacy of all our guests and is dedicated to protecting it. To enhance your experience on our website, we may ask you to provide certain information such as name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address. This information enables us to personalize your experience during your visit to our website.

We do not collect, save, or store any personal information from visitors who simply browse our website unless you voluntarily and intentionally provide it to us. We do not gather personal information without your knowledge or consent. Any personal information requested on our website is essential to provide specific services, such as processing your reservation.

For online reservations, we require contact information (such as name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address). This information allows us to reserve your stay and contact you if necessary. Rest assured, Him River Resort is committed to the security and confidentiality of the data collected on this website.

Hotel Policy

According to government regulations, a valid Photo ID has to be carried by every person above the age of 18 staying at the hotel. The identification proofs accepted are Drivers License, Voters Card, Passport, Ration Card. Without valid ID the guest will not be allowed to check in.

The primary guest checking in to the hotel must be at least 18 years of age. Children accompanying adults must be between 1-12 years.

The inclusion of extra bed with a booking is facilitated with a folding cot or a mattress as an extra bed.

Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may be chargeable by the hotel. The standard check-in time is 2 : 00 PM and the standard check-out time is 11 : 00 AM. After booking you will be sent an email confirmation with hotel phone number. You can contact the hotel directly for early check-in or late check-out.

The room tariff includes GST. The amount paid for the room does not include charges for optional services and facilities (such as room service, mini bar, snacks or telephone calls). These will be charged at the time of check-out.
