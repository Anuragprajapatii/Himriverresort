New Year Celebration In Rishikesh

**Celebrate New Year's Eve in Rishikesh with an Unforgettable DJ Night**
Welcome the New Year amidst the enchanting vibes of Rishikesh with an electrifying DJ night that promises to make your celebrations truly unforgettable.

Dance under the stars, surrounded by the serene beauty of the Himalayas, as pulsating beats and lively music fill the air. Join fellow revelers for a night of joy, laughter, and new beginnings, creating cherished memories against the backdrop of this spiritual haven. Celebrate the arrival of the New Year in style at Rishikesh's most vibrant party destination!

Your 2-night accommodation includes a stay in a luxury camp with an attached washroom King Size Bed, situated by the riverside and surrounded by nature. Enjoy provided the delicious 6 buffet meals, consisting of 2 breakfasts, 2 lunches (vegetarian), and 2 dinners (vegetarian/non-vegetarian).
