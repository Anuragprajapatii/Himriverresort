How To Reach Us

AIR

The nearest airport to Him River resort is Jolly Grant Airport, approximately 25 kilometers away and about a 50-minute drive. Its close proximity ensures convenient access for travelers flying in, making it a practical choice for arrival and departure.

BUS

The nearest bus stand to Him River Resort is Rishikesh bus stand, situated approximately 12 kilometers away, with a travel time of around 40 minutes by road. This proximity allows for convenient access to and from the resort for travelers using the bus network, ensuring easy connectivity to nearby areas.

TRAIN

The nearest train station to our resort is Yog Nagri Rishikesh, located 13 kilometers away and approximately a 40-minute drive. Its proximity makes it convenient for travelers arriving by train to reach us quickly and comfortably.
