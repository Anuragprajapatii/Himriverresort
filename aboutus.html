ABOUT US

Welcome to Him River Resort, nestled in the serene landscapes of Rishikesh, just 5 to 6 kilometers from the bustling heart of the city on Neelkanth Road. Established in 2012, our resort was founded with a singular purpose: to provide a tranquil haven where guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of nature.

Situated amidst the awe-inspiring backdrop of the Himalayas, Him River Resort offers a unique opportunity to experience the soothing presence of the Heul River. Originating from the Himalayas, this gentle river flows gracefully in front of our resort before merging with the sacred River Ganga, a mere 200 meters away.

At Him River Resort, we pride ourselves on offering not just accommodation, but an experience that rejuvenates the soul. Whether you seek adventure in the nearby hills or seek solace in the calming presence of the river, our resort promises a memorable stay for every guest.

Come, discover a place where tranquility meets adventure, where the mountains whisper tales of serenity, and the river hums melodies of peace. Him River Resort awaits to fulfill your desire for a blissful retreat amidst nature's bounty.

Indulge in a serene escape at our resort in Rishikesh, where we go beyond the ordinary to offer a host of amenities tailored to enrich your experience. As you arrive, our spacious parking facilities ensure a hassle-free start to your retreat. Inside, each room is equipped with modern conveniences including efficient air conditioning and heaters, promising comfort throughout your stay.

Come discover a haven where luxury meets nature, promising an unforgettable stay enriched with warmth, comfort, and endless possibilities for relaxation and enjoyment.
